On Revelation

October 23, 2014

I am essentially a chimpanzee and my various emotional states tend to reflect this. Hunger, anger, joy, and the like. However, occasionally I sit down and attempt to use my brain. Weird, right? Due to my animalistic nature though, usually my contemplative episodes focus on topics that I have a strong emotional attachment to. But the other day I had a slightly different thought:

"What can I do to improve my life?"

Strangely, the answer came to me almost instantly. So, I decided to act upon my well-timed revelation. I guess the point of this post is that, sometimes, a little alone time with our craniums is all that is needed to point us in the right direction. Even if aha! moments only happen a fraction of the time, what could possibly be lost by giving a little time to reflect on questions like these?
